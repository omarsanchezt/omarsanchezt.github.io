Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, our record store was built on a simple belief: music is meant to be held, heard, and experienced. In a world overflowing with digital streams and algorithm-driven playlists, we’re here to keep the magic of physical media alive. From the gentle crackle of a well-loved vinyl to the satisfying click of a cassette sliding into place, we celebrate the tangible connection that only real music formats can provide.

More than just a shop, we’re a community hub for San Luis Obispo’s music lovers. We host listening parties, local artist showcases, and events that bring people together to share the joy of discovering something new. Our team is made up of musicians, collectors, and lifelong fans who are always excited to talk tunes, recommend favorites, or help you track down that one album you can’t get out of your head.

At the end of the day, our mission is simple: to keep the spirit of physical media alive and thriving. We believe that music isn’t just something you hear—it’s something you feel, collect, display, and cherish. Here in SLO, we’re proud to provide a home for those who value the art, history, and heart behind every record we sell.
